RICHTAX

Wikidata reconciliation tool for TEI taxonomies

Welcome!

The Richtax App is a tool that enables you to easily enrich your TEI taxonomies with Linked Open Data references.

Let your taxonomy terms be compared to Wikidata entries and their descriptions, and choose a matching Wikidata item to be linked to your taxonomy entry in an easy-to-handle user interface. The application will enrich your taxonomy entries with Wikidata Q Identifiers - unique identifiers that will bring your taxonomy one step closer to being a part of the Semantic Web by using and meeting one of the principles of Linked Open Data.

It just takes a few simple steps to reconcile your data with:

  1. Copy and paste the taxonomy section of a valid TEI document or upload a valid TEI document that contains a taxonomy.
  2. The app then processes certain text data entries from your taxonomy and uses the Wikidata Query Service to find relevant information.
  3. For every entry in your taxonomy a selection of results will be presented - as far as there are any matches.
  4. You will find a list of matches for every entry and then may decide which Wikidata entry fits the best with your taxonomy entry.
  5. After selecting matching Wikidata entries you can download your taxonomy and copy a customized snippet of a prefix definition for the taxonomy.

To learn more about the Richtax App and how to make proper use of it, have a look at the Documentation.

If you are ready to give it a try, let's get started!
